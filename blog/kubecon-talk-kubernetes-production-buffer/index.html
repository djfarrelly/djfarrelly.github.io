KubeCon Talk: Kubernetes in Production at Buffer

November 20, 2016 by Dan Farrelly

At KubeCon 2016, I gave a talk about how we started using Kuberentes at Buffer including our learnings along the way. The talk was focused on what our initial challenges were, how Kubernetes would help us with those challenges and the plan that we executed to begin our transition to running applications and services in production on a Kubernetes cluster.
